This datasets provides various statistics on cancer cases such as age, type, location of victims, and numbre of cases. The age and location fields are the most likely be linked with other datasets to see if there are any contributing factors in demographics.

The secondary dataset is five tables for clinical trials related to solving cancer. 
The major areas where joins can happen are in either area or date. Both of these can be used 
to find intersections betweens groups that have cancer and clinical trials to cure it.
